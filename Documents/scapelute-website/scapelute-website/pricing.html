Questions

Good questions. Straight answers.

Do I own my website?

Yes, fully. Once you've paid in full, the website is yours — the code, the domain (if we registered it for you), everything. We don't hold your site hostage. If you ever want to move hosts or take over management, we hand everything over, no questions asked.

How long does it take?

5–10 business days from the moment you put down your deposit. We don't drag projects out. You'll have a site to review quickly, then we refine based on your feedback. Most clients are live in under two weeks.

What if I don't like it?

We revise until you do. Seriously. Your 50% deposit includes revision rounds — if the direction isn't right, we course-correct. The remaining 50% is only due when you're happy with the final result and ready to go live.

Do you do ongoing support?

Yes — it's included in our monthly hosting & maintenance plan ($40–$80/mo). That covers fast hosting, security monitoring, and monthly content updates. Need a new service added to your site? That's covered. Want to update your phone number? Done. You message us, we handle it.

Can I see examples first?

Absolutely. We have live demo sites built specifically for HVAC and plumbing contractors — real, working websites you can click through right now. Check the demos page and see exactly what you'd get before spending a dollar.
